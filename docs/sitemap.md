## Public Pages

- `/` - Home
- `/about` - About Us
- `/features` - Features overview and detail pages
- `/solution` - Solution pages for different user types
- `/pricing` - Pricing and subscription information
- `/market` - Market analysis and statistics
- `/resources` - Blog, case studies, guides, and templates
- `/community` - Community hub and events
- `/partners` - Partner information
- `/team` - Team and advisors
- `/contact` - Contact information and form
- `/login` - Authentication
- `/signup` - User registration

## Authenticated Pages (Dashboard)

All dashboard routes require authentication:

- `/dashboard` - Main dashboard overview
- `/dashboard/documents` - Document management
- `/dashboard/one-pager` - One-pager creation and management
- `/dashboard/ide-readiness` - IDE readiness assessment
- `/dashboard/analytics` - Performance analytics
- `/dashboard/financial` - Financial metrics
- `/dashboard/compliance` - Regulatory compliance
- `/dashboard/grant-advisor` - Grant advice chatbot
- `/dashboard/funding` - Funding opportunities
- `/dashboard/community` - Community features
- `/dashboard/startup-management` - Incubator-only startup management
- `/dashboard/settings` - User and company settings
- `/dashboard/support` - Help and support

## Key Features

### Document Management
Secure storage, verification, and categorization of startup documents across multiple categories (financial, legal, product, team, market research).

### One-Pager Dashboard
AI-powered one-pager generation with customizable templates, QR code sharing, and analytics tracking.

### IDE Readiness Assessment
Comprehensive readiness evaluation across four dimensions:
- Technology Readiness Level (TRL)
- Manufacturing Readiness Level (MRL)
- Commercial Readiness Level (CRL)
- Business Readiness Level (BRL)

### Grant Advice BOT
AI-powered chatbot for grant discovery, eligibility checking, and application guidance.

### Financial Metrics
Dashboard for tracking revenue, burn rate, runway, and key financial ratios.

### Regulatory Compliance
Checklists and templates for legal requirements and compliance management.
